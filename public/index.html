How to Import into Outlook

  1. Click "Download .ics" button above to save the calendar file.
  2. Open the downloaded .ics file (or double-click it).
  3. Outlook will prompt you to import the events into your calendar.
  4. Select your calendar and click "Import" or "OK".
  5. Your assignments, tests, and events will now appear in your Outlook calendar!
